Origins of the game


Rock paper scissors is a hand game usually played between two people, in which each player simultaneously forms one of three shapes with an outstretched hand. The type of game originated in China and spread with increased contact with East Asia, while developing different variants in signs over time. One of the variants that have become very popular in the last years is the one created by Sam Kass and Karen Bryle (add web ref: http://www.samkass.com/theories/RPSSL.html) and popularised by Sheldon Cooper from the well known series of the Big Bang Theory.
